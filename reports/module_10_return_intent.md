# Module 10: Return Intent Signals — Who Comes Back?

**Research Question:** From the qualitative text, who signals plans to return — and does this differ by age and experience level?

**Methodology:** Analysis of 724 Q5 responses from Transformation, Emotions/Experiences, and Beyond survey types (2024+2025), segmented by age group and Burn Status. Sample: Full Dataset.
