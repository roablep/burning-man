# Rising Sparks — Anti-Brand Guidelines

> These are **anti-brand guidelines** — a tool, not a dictating factor. Use this as a guiding framework that empowers creativity rather than constrains it. Ground your work in our core pillars and mission, then adapt boldly to your local context and voice.

---

## Identity

**Rising Sparks** is a grassroots collective of passionate individuals igniting the next generation of artists, leaders, changemakers, and innovators by stewarding an accessible, culturally diverse, and evolving creative community.

Rising Sparks is a symbol open for personal interpretation — a shared experience that gains power through subjective meaning, not rigid uniformity.

---

## Mission

> *Rising Sparks is a grassroots collective of passionate individuals igniting the next generation of artists, leaders, change makers, and innovators by stewarding an accessible, culturally diverse, and evolving creative community.*

---

## Vision

> *A thriving global network where communities co-create cultures rooted in shared values. Wisdom flows naturally between generations — creating together, learning together, evolving together.*

---

## Core Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Collaboration** | Intergenerational collaboration connecting established community builders with emerging leaders through structured mentorship and knowledge transfer |
| **Connection** | Serving as connective tissue that plugs next-gen into existing projects, communities, organizations, and volunteer opportunities — rather than creating parallel structures |
| **Innovation** | Championing next-generation innovation through experimentation, and empowering emerging leaders to take initiative |
| **Evolution** | Cultural co-creation and evolution that honors diverse traditions while actively participating in culture-making and supporting communities in developing their own cultural expressions |

## Logo

### Concept
The logomark features an iconic spark **"A"** — a geometric abstraction serving as both letter and stylized flame, representing ignition, regeneration, and resistance to commodification. The bold wordmark uses playful, hand-drawn letterforms with fluid, irregular edges that suggest movement and evolution.

### Usage Rules
- Display clearly against **black**, **white**, and **vibrant colored** backgrounds
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "A" on all sides
- Always test on your background for readability
- Rising Sparks is a trademarked name — the **™** symbol is required in official materials (soon)

### Do Not
- Distort, stretch, or alter the hand-drawn letterforms
- Remove the flame from the "A"
- Place the logo on busy backgrounds
- Add outlines, shadows, or effects
- Use the Burning Man logo without Tay's approval

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Spark Orange** | `#ff9200` | Emphasis and calls-to-action — use sparingly; represents ignition |
| **Charcoal Gray** | `#4a4a4a` | Body text and structure; grounds layouts for readability |
| **Warm Cream** | `#f7f3e9` | Primary background for ~70% of layouts |
| **Pure Black** | `#000000` | Maximum contrast for headlines |
| **Pure White** | `#ffffff` | Primary background (alongside Warm Cream) |

> Classic black and white are the primary colors, boldly contrasted by Spark Orange and Charcoal Gray to add energy, warmth, and movement.

## Typography

### Fonts

| Font | Role | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| **Anton Regular** | Headlines, section headers, titles | Keep short and punchy at large sizes; use minimally for maximum contrast |
| **Merriweather** | Body copy | Regular weight for paragraphs; bold for emphasis; never all-caps |

### Usage Guidelines
- Create hierarchy through **size variation**, not by adding more fonts
- Maintain generous line spacing for comfortable reading
- The Anton/Merriweather pairing creates dynamic contrast between geometric display and flowing serif — energetic yet grounded
- Test legibility at actual output size before finalizing

---

## Photography Style

- Use **authentic photography** shot on real cameras and film — no trendy filters or staged content
- Visuals should be **clean yet raw** — show rather than tell through genuine moments
- **Photo credits are mandatory** — include photographer credit under each photo or in end credits. Never use photos without attribution.

### Capture
- Community gatherings and creative work
- Intergenerational collaboration
- Next-generation voices and leadership
- Candid, authentic moments showing environmental context

### Avoid
- Stock photography
- Overly polished aesthetics
- Staged poses
