# Module 6: Playa Love vs. Default Love (Relationships)

**Research Question:** How do relationship dynamics and intimacy differ in the temporary autonomous zone?

**Methodology:** Separate analyses of Q5 (new love), Q6 (committed relationships), and Q7 (playa vs. default comparison). Results are segmented by year and burn status where possible.

> **Note:** 2025 sample size is much smaller; avoid unweighted cross-year comparisons.

## Q5: Meeting Someone New and Falling in Love

- **Experienced Love on Playa:** 18.2%
- **Top Relationship Type:** Unclear
- **Top Themes:** Connection, Novelty, Uncertainty, Freedom
- **Tone Split:** Neutral (99), Positive (23), Negative (11)
