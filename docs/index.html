Burning Man Research Hub

An analysis of participant census data and field notes from 2024–2025.

5,028 weighted census respondents · 11.5% under 30 · 73.9% in placed camps · 154 field note transformation narratives

Two parallel research workstreams — a quantitative cohort analysis of the 2025 weighted census and a qualitative thematic analysis of 2024–25 field notes — surface patterns in who comes to Burning Man, who stays, and what the experience means to them.
